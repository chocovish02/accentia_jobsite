About Us

Founded in 2015, Accentia Technologies is a trusted partner for global enterprises in digital transformation. Our core areas include Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Architecture, Cybersecurity, and Enterprise Automation. Headquartered in Bangalore, with R&D centers in Pune and Frankfurt, we serve clients across 14+ countries.

Our clients include Fortune 500 companies, early-stage startups, and government institutions. Accentia is ISO 27001 and SOC 2 certified, ensuring compliance and excellence in every solution we build.
